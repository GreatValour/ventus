Overview

  • Ventus seeks out brilliant, enthusiastic individuals who share our values and desire for success, and we provide them with the support and flexibility they need to excel at work, at home, and in their communities. We provide all of this in an open-door, collaborative environment that promotes and honors teamwork and individual contributions. You will be given the opportunity to be a part of our team, deeply involved with small to medium-sized businesses and wealthy individuals. We offer independence for business owners, thus we require someone who is growth-oriented, able to provide intelligent advice to our small and medium-sized businesses. To be successful, you must be able to conduct research that goes beyond numbers, possess clarity and purpose for your career, and strive for performance. We exist for the sole purpose of increasing our client's profitability and business value, thus your vision must be consistent with ours. We are searching for students that are eager to embark on challenging projects and get experience with a variety of industry activities. All majors are encouraged to apply. Internships are part-time (15 hours a week) during the school year and full-time (40 hours a week) during the summer.

    • Responsibilities

    1. Assist with financial analysis, reporting, and reconciliation processes in the finance department.
    2. Implementation of ACH payments for merchants.
    3. Participate in the preparation of financial statements, invoices of Merchant accounts.
    4. Accounts Payable: Assists with reconciling and preparing merchant accounts for processing.
    5. Assists with the review and processing of Merchant related invoices, ensuring charge codes and Merchant's funding has been verified.
    6. New Merchant set-up and maintenance in the system.
    7. Invoicing support - includes gathering supporting documentation.
    8. Utilizes various systems and tools, performs research and assists with implementations as needed.
    9. Performs tasks that help in learning the business area and which provide an overall view of company operations.
    10. Assist other staff members with everyday tasks to gain an understanding of the Company's operations and workflow.
    11. Completes other assigned Department related tasks.

  • High school diploma or equivalent: Possession of a high school diploma or equivalent educational qualification.

    6. Must be able to exercise discretion with confidential information, strong attention to detail.
